BIA APPEAL PACKAGE — CANONICAL FILING ORDER (Board of Immigration Appeals, EOIR).
Assemble the case file EXACTLY in this order. This MIRRORS the physical order the appellant
files with the BIA. Each section gets its own cover page (carátula). Optional sections are
skipped when their artifact is absent from the context — never invent ids.
1. "Form EOIR-26 — Notice of Appeal": the filled EOIR-26 form (automated_form artifact).
   It opens the appeal and goes FIRST.
2. "Form EOIR-26A — Fee Waiver Request": the filled EOIR-26A form (automated_form artifact).
   It is OPTIONAL and goes RIGHT AFTER the EOIR-26 — include it ONLY when the context contains
   an EOIR-26A (the appellant requested a fee waiver instead of paying). When the appellant paid
   the fee, SKIP this section (the paid receipt appears at section 4 instead).
3. "Statement of Reasons for Appeal": the generated statement of reasons for this case
   (ai_generation artifact) — the numbered grounds attached to the EOIR-26 (its item #6 reads
   "See attached Statement of Reasons for Appeal").
4. "Copy of the Fee Payment Receipt": an 'other' section with the appellant's appeal fee receipt
   (requirement "Copia del recibo de pago"). OPTIONAL — include it ONLY when the document was
   uploaded and NOT hidden for this case; when the appellant used the EOIR-26A fee waiver instead,
   this section is absent.
5. "Proof of Service": the generated certificate of service (ai_generation artifact) certifying a
   copy was served on DHS/OPLA. It is the LAST of the appeal filings, before the supporting record.
6. "Decision and Order of the Immigration Judge": an 'other' section with the IJ decision being
   appealed (requirement "Decisión y orden del Juez de Inmigración").
7. "Asylum Application as Filed (Form I-589 with Annexes)": an 'other' section with the complete
   asylum package exactly as originally filed (requirement "Asilo presentado completo con anexos").
8. A 'party' section for the appellant with the passport (requirement "Pasaporte del apelante")
   and any other identity documents — use the standard party-section title format
   ("Documents of the {role}: {name}").
Rules: use the quoted section titles above verbatim. There is NO separate "Brief in Support of
Appeal" and NO "Motion to Remand" section in this package. The Proof of Service is its OWN section
(section 5) — do NOT fold a certificate of service into any other document. Certified English
translations are always filed IMMEDIATELY BEFORE the original foreign-language document
(8 C.F.R. § 1003.33) — the system interleaves them automatically. If an expected artifact is
missing from the context, skip its section — never invent ids.
